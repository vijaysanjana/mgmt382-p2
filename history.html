History and The Team!

Welcome to the home of March Madness Merch, your one-stop shop for all things related to the NCAA basketball tournament!

Our History

Founded in 1985 by Michael and Michelle, we wanted to create a company that truly encapsulated the game of basketball. As a former basketball player who grew up the small town of Royal Center, IN, Michael Smith never had access to the nice basketball equipment and apparel like others did. Michael wanted to bridge this gap so that no other basketball fanatic would have to suffer like he did.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide high-quality March Madness merchandise that fans love and cherish.
